What You Can Do

Become an Activist

If there is an important cause that is worth becoming an activist for it is this one. Join non-profit groups that plant trees, help wildlife and support bills that help save the planet!

Stop Supporting Climate Deniers

There are too many politcians that do not believe that climate change is a serious issue or are bought by the fossil fuel industry, it is time to stop supporting them and vote in people who care about this issue and want to help

Support Green Technology

There are so many new technologies like EV cars and more effiencent machines that use less gas, that can solve our dependence on fossil fuels and help prevent climate change, it is important to get involved and make a career that helps develop these technologies

EMBRACE NATURE

Step outside into nature and feel the breeze, the sun, hear the sounds of a river or an owl overhead, this is what is at stake if we do not step up and change our ways. It is important to feel gratitude to nature and it will also help motivate you to make a difference!

GREEN LIFESTYLE

Develop habits that can save the planet! Plant trees and join groups that do planting excavations, it is also wise to recycle more often and use products that use less plastics and products that are harmful to the planet, try to convince your friends that they can make a difference too!

SUPPORT LEGISLATION

It is a good idea to join groups that support bills like Green New Deal that will create green investment and jobs while paving the way for new green technology, protest politcians who are not in favor of these bills and try to convince them otherwise. There are many groups such as Sunrise Movement that can help you get your foot in the door!
